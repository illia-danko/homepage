such as [[https://www.omnisend.com/][Omnisend]] and
[[https://aigent.ai/][Aigent]].

He is always looking at optimizing workflow and developing helping tools. As a
Free Software consumer occasionally contributes further back to Open Source.
Reads novels and tech books and tries out new stuff in his spare time.
