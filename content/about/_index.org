Illia Danko is a passionate software engineer, beloved husband, and father of
three kids. He started his career as a system developer around nine years ago,
and gradually has been moving towards Web, and now positioning himself as a full
stack engineer. He has solid experience at big tech companies such as
[[https://www.cisco.com/][Cisco]], as well as at startups, such as
[[https://www.omnisend.com/][Omnisend]] and [[https://aigent.ai/][Aigent]].

He is always looking at optimizing workflow and developing helping tools. As a
Free Software consumer, he occasionally contributes further back to Open Source.
He reads novels and tech books and tries new stuff in his spare time.
